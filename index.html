I work for Docker, bringing the benefits of containers to developers everywhere. Previously I worked for Unikernel Systems (techcrunch), improving application performance and security using type-safe languages and virtual library operating systems (ACM queue, USENIX).

For maximum impact I enjoy working on open-source projects (e.g. Mirage OS, Xen) and commercial software (e.g. Docker Desktop based on Mirage OS and XenServer based on Xen). I have a PhD from the University of Cambridge Computer Laboratory and was a consultant research scientist at Fraser Research in Princeton. While teaching and studying, I co-founded High Energy Magic and shipped mobile apps which could interact with printed visual symbols known as SpotCodes.
