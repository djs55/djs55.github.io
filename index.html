I work for Docker, bringing the benefits of containers to developers everywhere. Previously I worked for Unikernel Systems (techcrunch), improving application performance and security using type-safe languages and virtual library operating systems (ACM queue, USENIX).

Highlighted publications

ICFP 2025: Bringing Functional Programming to Container Technology
CACM 2014: Unikernels - Library Operating Systems for the Cloud
IEEE 2005: Early Smartphone Research and Mobile Computing
IEEE 2005: Audio Processing and Digital Signal Processing

For maximum impact I enjoy working on open-source projects and commercial software together; for example Docker Desktop is based on Mirage OS and XenServer is based on Xen. I co-founded High Energy Magic and shipped mobile apps which "connected Paper and Online Worlds" (New York Times, MIT Technology Review, Wired) via printed visual symbols known as SpotCodes. I have a PhD from the University of Cambridge Computer Laboratory and was a consultant research scientist at Fraser Research in Princeton working on next-generation Internet architecture funded by the NSF.
